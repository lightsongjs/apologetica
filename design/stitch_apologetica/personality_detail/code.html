King of Salem, Priest of God Most High

history_edu

Bio

A mysterious figure in the Old Testament, Melchisedec is both a king and a priest who appears briefly in Genesis to bless Abraham. His name means 'King of Righteousness,' and he is recognized as a profound archetype of an eternal priesthood, having neither beginning of days nor end of life.
